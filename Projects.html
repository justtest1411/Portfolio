PROJECTS

Project 1: Smart Home Automation System

Developed a smart home automation system using Arduino and IoT technology. The system allows users to control home appliances remotely via a mobile app, enhancing convenience and energy efficiency.

Project 2: Renewable Energy Integration in Power Grids

Conducted research on integrating renewable energy sources into existing power grids. Proposed a model for optimizing energy distribution and reducing reliance on fossil fuels, contributing to sustainable energy solutions.

Project 3: Electric Vehicle Charging Station Design

Designed an electric vehicle charging station with efficient power management and user-friendly interface. The project involved circuit design, software development, and testing to ensure reliable performance.
